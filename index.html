Why Written Commitment Works
Writing activates encoding processes distinct from verbal thought. The act of translating intention to text increases accountability — there is now a record your future self is answerable to. Studies in implementation intention research show written pre-commitment reduces impulsive deviation by 30–50% in high-stakes tasks.
